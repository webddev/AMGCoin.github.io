Token Description

What is AMG Coin?

Amg coin (Amalgama) is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that makes it easy to transfer money online. Think of it as "Internet currency."

What is Amalgama?

"Amalgam" means "alloy" in Latin! In fact, it is an alloy of several metals, which can withstand enormous stresses and yet remain very flexible!

Very "friendly" and incredibly strong!

AMG coin differs from other digital currencies by its hardness and friendliness! Due to the hardness of amalgam it will always hold its price, and due to the flexibility of amalgam it can be friendly to both beginners and professionals!

How to get started with AMG coin.

Creating an AMG wallet is as easy as counting to 3. Seriously! You can buy or exchange it on the exchange, and then withdraw it to your wallet. Once you're done, join the fun in our growing community! That's where we run the contest! We're a friendly bunch and will always answer any questions you ask us!
